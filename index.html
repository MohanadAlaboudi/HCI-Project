About Our Mission

This project aims to bridge the knowledge gap about disabilities by building a simple, educational, and accessible website that presents information about disabilities in a clear and friendly way.

Our goal is to raise awareness about different types of disabilities and provide useful guidance on how to communicate, interact, and assist people with various conditions.
