About Our Project

Promoting understanding, empathy, and inclusion through accessible education

Our Mission

Many individuals lack knowledge about disabilities, leading to social and communication challenges. This project aims to bridge this gap by building a simple, educational, and accessible website that presents information about disabilities in a clear and friendly way.

Project Objectives

  • Educate the public about disabilities and promote empathy and inclusion
  • Present information in a structured, organized, and visually clear format
  • Follow accessibility standards and HCI design principles
  • Build a responsive and easy-to-navigate web interface
  • Support both Arabic and English content in future versions

Our Approach

The website focuses on inclusivity, user experience, and accessibility. By applying WCAG 2.1 standards and HCI guidelines, the system ensures that everyone — including users with disabilities — can benefit equally from the platform.

Future Vision

We plan to expand this concept into a larger community-driven platform that encourages understanding, collaboration, and equality for all individuals, regardless of their physical or cognitive abilities.
